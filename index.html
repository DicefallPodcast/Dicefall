Dicefall

A D&D 5E Live Podcast


A 5th edition Dungeons and Dragons real play podcast. Anyone is welcome to enjoy our games but we mainly created this podcast for the fun of re-listening to our own game sessions. These episodes are a full recordings of our weekly D&D game session. We don't do much sound adjustments or editing. We are also adults and an occasional swear may slip out.
The first campaign, Vengeance of the Lost, is set in the world of Ethea. This is a home brew campaign.
The second campaign is an adaption of Tyranny of Dragons. I am adding some content and this version has 3 moons - Iustitia, the white moon (good), Aequalis, the red moon (neutral) and Infortunii, the black moon (evil) - that affect the magic in the world.
